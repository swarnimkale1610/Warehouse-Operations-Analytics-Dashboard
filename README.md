# 📌 Project Overview

The **Warehouse Operations Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze warehouse operations, inventory movement, customer orders, sales performance, payment trends, and employee productivity.

The dashboard enables managers and business stakeholders to monitor operational KPIs, identify bottlenecks, and make data-driven decisions using interactive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor warehouse performance
- Track sales and customer orders
- Analyze payment methods
- Evaluate employee productivity
- Identify top-performing categories and regions
- Improve operational efficiency through data-driven insights

---

# 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 📦 Total Orders | Number of customer orders |
| 💰 Total Sales | Overall revenue generated |
| 👥 Total Customers | Number of unique customers |
| 📦 Total Quantity Sold | Total products sold |
| ⭐ Average Rating | Customer satisfaction score |
| 🚚 Average Delivery Days | Average delivery time |

---

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Electronics category generated the highest sales.
- Delivered orders account for the majority of total orders.
- UPI and Card are the most preferred payment methods.
- South region contributes the highest revenue.
- Average delivery time remains around 4 days.
- Employee productivity can be compared using processed orders.
